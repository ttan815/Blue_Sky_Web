About Us

We humbly invite you to our little corner of Carmichael. Our wonderful chefs prepare delicious Chinese food so you can enjoy a quite and relaxing meal. Our Mandarin, Hunan, and Szechuan Cuisine dishes are bound to satisfy your hunger. We also prepare food for pick-up. You may conveniently order over the phone, fax, or email pick it up and enjoy it anywhere! We also do catering for most occasions, family gatherings or office parties. Order in advance and we can deliver for your party. We'd love to see you and share with you the flavors of Chinese cuisine. Family Friendly Dining Experience!
